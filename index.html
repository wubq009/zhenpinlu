Group Introduction

Research in the Lu group involves various aspects of organic, organometallic, main-group, supramolecular, and materials chemistry. We aim to design and synthesize new main-group element-based compounds (with a particular interest in boron) that may have potential applications in organic synthesis, small molecular activation, catalysis, host-guest chemistry, and optoelectronic devices. We solve complicated problems, understand reaction mechanisms, and design new molecules through a combination of computation chemistry and experimental results.

Research Areas

Main-group Chemistry

The design and synthesis of main-group element compounds, particularly boron-based compounds, explores their applications in organic synthesis, small molecule activation, and catalysis.

Supramolecular Chemistry

The development of supramolecular systems focuses on their functional applications, including self-assembly, host-guest chemistry, and molecular recognition.

Organometallic Chemistry

The mechanistic investigation of organometallic structure-reactivity paradigms directs their tailored implementation in functional materials design and catalytic transformations.

Contact Us

We are always looking for highly motivated students/scientists to join our group. If you are interested in working in our group, please get in touch (zhenpilu@cityu.edu.hk).

Ph.D. Positions

We have open Ph.D. positions annually, usually starting in September, January or June. If you are interested, please get in touch as early as possible. For your application, please send a CV, cover letter, proof of English proficiency (TOEFL 80 or IELTS Band 6.5 or CET-6 492), and transcripts of your previous studies to Dr. Lu.

Postdoctoral / Research assistant positions

Scientists with a good tracking record in organic synthesis and organometallic chemistry are encouraged to apply for our postdoctoral/research assistant positions.
